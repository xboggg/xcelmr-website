Our Story

Delivering Excellence, One Service at a Time

Xcel Services Ltd was founded with a clear mission: to be Ghana's premier multi-service solutions provider. We recognized that businesses need reliable, efficient, and high-quality support across various operational needs, and we set out to deliver exactly that.

Today, we are proud to offer a diverse portfolio of services including Football Shootout experiences, professional Ushering, Sunny Holidays travel coordination, skilled Mechanic services, and comprehensive Facilities Management. Our commitment to professionalism, adaptability, and client-focused approach has earned us the trust of leading event centres and businesses across Ghana.
